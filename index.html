Passive Vortex Propulsion

Self-built wind tunnel · CFD · published research

I built a wind tunnel from scratch to test an idea about aerodynamic drag — soldered and calibrated the load cells with an Arduino and ran the CFD in Fusion 360. It was the first demonstration of passive vortex propulsion in air.
